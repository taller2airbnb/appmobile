Sign In

You will be greeted by the sign in screen. To sign in to the app, enter your username and password, then press the sign in button. Alternatively, you can use the "Sign in with Google" button to use your phone's Google account.

If you don't have a user account yet, you can create one by clicking the Register button.

Enter your personal information as well as a password, which must be 8 characters or more. You can choose whether to make a Guest or Host account.

 

Password Recovery

If you can't remember your password, or want to change it, click on the "Forgot your Password?" button on the sign in screen.

Enter your email, and press the get token button. A token will be sent to your email. Copy the token, then on this screen, press the "use token" button.

On this screen, enter you email, token, and new password. Then press the "Change Password" button and your password will be changed. You can press the "Get Token" button to return to the previous screen, or "Sign In" to go back to the sign in screen.

 

Navigation

Once you have signed in correctly, you can navigate the app by pulling the sidebar from the left edge of the screen at any time. The available screens may be different for each user type.

 

My Profile

All users can access the "My Profile" screen from the navigation sidebar. This screen lists you name, username, email, national id number and type, and your wallet id (which can be copied to clipboard when pressed).

Clicking on the "Edit Profile" button allows users to modify their name and Id numbers.

 

Search Postings

Guest users can use this screen (accessed from the navigation bar) to look for postings to book. The postings can be filtered:

  • By location: select country and city.
  • By date: select check in and check out date.
  • By features: click on the desired features.
  • By number of guests: will filter out postings with a lower maximum amount of guests.
  • By price: select minimum and maximum price in Ethereum.

Press the "Apply Filters" button to start your search. Alternatively you can press the "Hosts near me" button.

Simply tap on the posting you're interested in.

 

Browsing a posting

There are several tabs on a posting to give potential guests more information.

  • Pictures: Pictures of the posting.
  • Description: a brief description of the post.
  • Features: list of features of the post.
  • Reviews: reviews submitted by previous guests.
  • Comments: public comments left by guests and the owner.

To make a check in request, enter the check in and check out date. The price for your full stay will be listed below. Press the "Book Now" button to send the request for the host to approve.

If you press the Heart button, you will favorite this post, and it will appear on the "Favorites" screen that can be accessed from the navigation sidebar.

Additionally, you will get other recommended postings below.

 

Reviews

Once you have visited a posting, as a guest, you can leave a review. Choose from 1 to 5 stars and write down a comment. Everyone browsing the posting can see your review.

If you are host instead, you can rate your bookers by opening their profile.

 

Comments and chat

There's a tab named "Comment" on postings. There, multiple potential guests can leave public comments, which the user can also reply to.

The posting also allows you to talk to the posting's owner in private, as well as browsing their profile. Just click the "chat with Owner" button and you will be sent to a private chat room.

Once you've chatted with the owner, he will be added as a contact. You can access your contacts on the "Contact" tab of the navigation sideview and resume multiple conversations.

 

My Bookings

As a guest, if the host has accepted your booking request, you will be sent a notification and the posting will be added to the "My Bookings" screen, accessible from the navigation sidebar. If your booking has not yet been approved, it will show up in the "Pending" tab.

 

Create Posting

As a host, the "Create Posting" screen will be accessible from the navigation sidebar. This will allow you to create a posting, providing a name, description, location, maximum number of guests, prive per day in ETH, validity period, and features.

 

My Postings

As a host, your created postings can be seen from the "My Postings" screen in the navigation sidebar.

The tab on top of the screen allows you to toggle between Edit and View mode. In Edit mode, clicking on one of your postings will allow you to edit the posting.

On "View" mode, you will be able to view your own posting as if you were a guest user. You can view ratings and reply to comments, but you cannot post a review yourself, or book and favorite your own posting, or chat with yourself.

 

My Offers

As a host, you can see Offers made by guests on the "My Offers" screen, again, accessible from the navigation sidebar. You can accept or reject the offers.
